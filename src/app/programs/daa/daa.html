The benefits of weekly medicines pack from your pharmacist

Medicines are a health tool that are used to improve or maintain health. Sometimes medicines can be difficult to manage, especially when taking a number of medicines.

Managing yours or a family member's medicines can be made simple with help from your Pharmacist:

  • Every week your pharmacist can arrange all the medicines into a "Medicine Pack".
  • The medicines are neatly and safely organised by day and time of day.

That way, it's easier that medicines are being taken as intended by the Doctor
