Glycosylated Haemoglobin (HbA1c) Measurements

The HbA1c test is not the same as a Blood Glucose Level test. While your BGL can provide a snapshot of your immediate condition, your HbA1c gives a better overall picture of your condition as it reflects your average blood glucose levels over the past 10-12 weeks.
The HbA1c target range for most people with diabetes will be between 6.5-7% (48-53mmol/mol) but your doctor may choose a different target that is more personalised and appropriate for you.
